The app uses essential browser storage for session and draft behavior. Optional analytics only load after the user explicitly consents.

Essential storage

Theme preference, local draft recovery, and consent preferences are kept locally so the app can function and the user experience stays consistent.

Optional categories

If you accept optional cookies, Google Tag Manager may load Google Analytics so the site owner can understand traffic and usage patterns. If you choose essential-only cookies, those analytics tags stay off.

Consent

Users can choose essential-only storage or accept optional categories. Consent is stored locally in your browser so the site can remember your choice on future visits.

Changing your choice

You can reopen Cookie settings at any time and switch back to essential-only. When consent is withdrawn, future analytics loading is disabled and analytics cookies for this site are cleared where possible in the browser.
